Thank you as well to George Chen's suggestion of adding a note regarding the use of the Chinese hexagram symbols on the pages of Fiction, Poetry, Essay, Literary Thoughts, and Miscellaneous on this website. Adding these symbols is intended to promote general interest for the richness of traditional Chinese culture. These hexagram symbols originated in the classic text I Ching/Yijing 易经 (Book of Changes) represent profound philosophical concepts that have continued being studied and practiced in contemporary Chinese communities around the world. Correspondingly, including these complex hexagrams on this website serves as a way to encourage close reading of the selected works to appreciate the literary prolificacy, aesthetic quality, and historical value of James Poy Wong's writings.
