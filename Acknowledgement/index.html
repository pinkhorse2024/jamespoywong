Besides a few pictures of Wong that are taken from personal collection, other images included in the website contain proper source citation or are those personally created from Creative Commons images.

I would also like to note that the Chinese hexagram symbols used on the pages of Fiction, Poetry, Essay, Literary Thoughts, and Miscellaneous on this website are intended to promote general interest for the richness of traditional Chinese culture. These hexagram symbols originated in the classic text I Ching/Yijing 易经 （Book of Changes that represent profound philosophical concepts that have continued to be studied and applied in contemporary Chinese communities around the world. Correspondingly, these hexagrams are employed in in website to suggest close reading of the selected work to appreciate the literary prolificacy, aesthetic quality, and historical value of James Poy Wong's writings.
