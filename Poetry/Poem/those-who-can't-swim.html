James Poy Wong 黃培正

“The snow that has frozen sound and light keeps falling and falling"

"Those Who Can't Swim" - 1976

无能游泳的人


无痛中感受着体内每一部分的死
封固了声光的雪降了又降
无能游泳的人凭什么冲动
敢不忍看爱的夭折和人性的毁灭
等明天又要浮上来朝向太阳

Those Who Can't Swim


Feeling every part inside the body dying in painlessness
The snow that has frozen sound and light keeps falling and falling
Those who can’t swim cannot rely on impulsion
Dare to bear seeing death of a premature love and destruction of human nature
Waiting to float up again tomorrow to face the sun
